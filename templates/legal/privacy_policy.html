Privacy & Data Policy

{{ site_name }} — Gate & Attendance System

What data we collect

We collect and store:

  • Student identity: ID number, name, email, photo (optional), address, birthdate, guardian contact — for identification and QR-based gate/event attendance.
  • Gate and event records: Timestamps of scans (in/out), result (granted/denied), device ID, and who recorded the scan — for audit and reports.

Why we use it

Data is used to operate campus gate access, record event attendance, produce reports for administration, and comply with audit requirements.

Who can access it

  • Admin and staff: Full access to data, reports, and exports.
  • Guards: Can scan and view gate activity; only minimal data (e.g. ID, time, status) is shown on the guard screen.
  • Students: When an account is linked, students can view only their own gate and attendance records.

How long we keep it

We keep gate and attendance logs for a defined period (e.g. 1–2 years) for audit; older data may be archived or removed according to school policy. Student profiles are retained while enrolled; deactivated students are not scanned for new events but records may be kept for compliance.

Security

We use role-based access, secure connections (HTTPS in production), and do not expose your data to unauthenticated users. Database access is restricted to the application.

Your choices

To request deactivation of your record (no new scans), contact the administrator. Existing logs may be retained for audit.
